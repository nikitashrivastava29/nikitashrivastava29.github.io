Sophia



As a unique combination of science, engineering, and artistry, Sophia is simultaneously a human-crafted science fiction character depicting the future of AI and robotics, and a platform for advanced robotics and AI research.For example, she has been used for research as part of the Loving AI project, which seeks to understand how robots can adapt to users’ needs through intra and interpersonal development. Sophia can understand you and express emotions, but programmed. ( Click on the img for more info about Sophia! )

Astro - Home Assistant



The Amazon Astro is new to the market and shows promise to revolutionize the home monitoring industry. This little gadget is made to work with Alexa, so you can have voice-activation any time. Keeping track of Zoom meetings, when your favorite shows are on, and other scheduling concerns will be right at your fingertips. Additionally, the Amazon Astro offers simple to use privacy settings to that you can turn the cameras and recordings off at any time. ( Click on the img to get to know more about Astro! )
