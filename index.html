The World Of AI

Artificial intelligence is intelligence demonstrated by machines, as opposed to natural intelligence displayed by animals including humans.They have been programmed or coded to respond to humans and give them information in their reach to make our lives easier.

There are many AI bots out there. Many have been created to accustom to your lifestyle to make your daily tasks easier. While some have been made for more technical reasons, as machinery etc. Let us check out some examples of AI bots that are heavily used or interviewed.


Why Is AI important?

AI technology is important because it enables human capabilities – understanding, reasoning, planning, communication and perception – to be undertaken by software increasingly effectively, efficiently and at low cost.

How Is AI Made?

AI works by combining large amounts of data with fast, iterative processing and intelligent algorithms, allowing the software to learn automatically from patterns or features in the data. The process requires multiple passes at the data to find connections and derive meaning from undefined data.
